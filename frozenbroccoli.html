Broccoli

Broccoli

Our IQF Broccoli is carefully sourced from clean, sound crops. After harvest, the broccoli is sorted, cut to the required diameter, blanched, cooled, and frozen using the air fluidization technique. The product is then sorted to ensure the correct size, metal detected, and packed to meet strict quality standards. No additives are used in the manufacturing process, and the product is free from genetically modified techniques.

  • Grade: A
  • Origin: Egypt
  • Shapes: Florets, Crumbled
  • Sizes: [20:40] - [30:50] - [40:60]mm
  • Glazing: 5% - 10% - 15%
  • Season: Dec - Jan
  • Cutting: Manual
  • Packaging options:
    • Bulk: 10 Kg / 30 lbs
    • Retail: 1 Kg / 2 Kg / 2.5 Kg / 5 Kg
  • Totbins: 350 Kg
